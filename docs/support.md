    <div class="mb-12">
      <h2 class="text-2xl font-bold text-fieldpad-900 mb-8 text-center">Frequently Asked Questions</h2>

      <div class="space-y-6">

        <details class="group bg-white border border-gray-200 rounded-xl overflow-hidden">
          <summary class="flex items-center justify-between cursor-pointer px-6 py-4 font-semibold text-fieldpad-900 hover:bg-gray-50 transition-colors">
            What is FieldPad?
            <svg class="w-5 h-5 text-gray-400 group-open:rotate-180 transition-transform" fill="none" stroke="currentColor" viewBox="0 0 24 24"><path stroke-linecap="round" stroke-linejoin="round" stroke-width="2" d="M19 9l-7 7-7-7"/></svg>
          </summary>
          <div class="px-6 pb-4 text-gray-600 text-sm leading-relaxed">
            FieldPad is an offline-first iOS app for HVAC and refrigeration technicians. It tracks EPA Section 608 and AIM Act Subpart C compliance, calculates leak rates, manages A2L safety checklists, handles refrigerant cylinder inventory with OCR scanning, and generates professional invoices with digital signatures — all without requiring an internet connection.
          </div>
        </details>

        <details class="group bg-white border border-gray-200 rounded-xl overflow-hidden">
          <summary class="flex items-center justify-between cursor-pointer px-6 py-4 font-semibold text-fieldpad-900 hover:bg-gray-50 transition-colors">
            Does FieldPad work offline?
            <svg class="w-5 h-5 text-gray-400 group-open:rotate-180 transition-transform" fill="none" stroke="currentColor" viewBox="0 0 24 24"><path stroke-linecap="round" stroke-linejoin="round" stroke-width="2" d="M19 9l-7 7-7-7"/></svg>
          </summary>
          <div class="px-6 pb-4 text-gray-600 text-sm leading-relaxed">
            Yes. FieldPad is built offline-first. All core features — job management, compliance logging, leak rate calculations, invoicing, OCR scanning, and PDF generation — work without an internet connection. Data syncs automatically via iCloud when connectivity is available.
          </div>
        </details>

        <details class="group bg-white border border-gray-200 rounded-xl overflow-hidden">
          <summary class="flex items-center justify-between cursor-pointer px-6 py-4 font-semibold text-fieldpad-900 hover:bg-gray-50 transition-colors">
            Is FieldPad free?
            <svg class="w-5 h-5 text-gray-400 group-open:rotate-180 transition-transform" fill="none" stroke="currentColor" viewBox="0 0 24 24"><path stroke-linecap="round" stroke-linejoin="round" stroke-width="2" d="M19 9l-7 7-7-7"/></svg>
          </summary>
          <div class="px-6 pb-4 text-gray-600 text-sm leading-relaxed">
            FieldPad offers a free tier with limits (5 clients, 10 jobs, 10 invoices, 20 compliance logs, 15 OCR scans, 8 PDF generations). FieldPad Pro removes all limits and unlocks features like data export, recurring jobs, business logo on invoices, and unlimited OCR scanning. All existing data remains accessible if you downgrade.
          </div>
        </details>

        <details class="group bg-white border border-gray-200 rounded-xl overflow-hidden">
          <summary class="flex items-center justify-between cursor-pointer px-6 py-4 font-semibold text-fieldpad-900 hover:bg-gray-50 transition-colors">
            How do I cancel or manage my subscription?
            <svg class="w-5 h-5 text-gray-400 group-open:rotate-180 transition-transform" fill="none" stroke="currentColor" viewBox="0 0 24 24"><path stroke-linecap="round" stroke-linejoin="round" stroke-width="2" d="M19 9l-7 7-7-7"/></svg>
          </summary>
          <div class="px-6 pb-4 text-gray-600 text-sm leading-relaxed">
            Subscriptions are managed through your Apple ID. Go to <strong>Settings &gt; [Your Name] &gt; Subscriptions</strong> on your iPhone or iPad to view, modify, or cancel your FieldPad Pro subscription. Your data is never deleted when you cancel — you retain full access to view and edit existing records.
          </div>
        </details>

        <details class="group bg-white border border-gray-200 rounded-xl overflow-hidden">
          <summary class="flex items-center justify-between cursor-pointer px-6 py-4 font-semibold text-fieldpad-900 hover:bg-gray-50 transition-colors">
            How do I request a refund?
            <svg class="w-5 h-5 text-gray-400 group-open:rotate-180 transition-transform" fill="none" stroke="currentColor" viewBox="0 0 24 24"><path stroke-linecap="round" stroke-linejoin="round" stroke-width="2" d="M19 9l-7 7-7-7"/></svg>
          </summary>
          <div class="px-6 pb-4 text-gray-600 text-sm leading-relaxed">
            Refunds are handled by Apple. Visit <a href="https://reportaproblem.apple.com" class="text-fieldpad-600 hover:underline">reportaproblem.apple.com</a> to request a refund for any App Store purchase.
          </div>
        </details>

        <details class="group bg-white border border-gray-200 rounded-xl overflow-hidden">
          <summary class="flex items-center justify-between cursor-pointer px-6 py-4 font-semibold text-fieldpad-900 hover:bg-gray-50 transition-colors">
            Where is my data stored?
            <svg class="w-5 h-5 text-gray-400 group-open:rotate-180 transition-transform" fill="none" stroke="currentColor" viewBox="0 0 24 24"><path stroke-linecap="round" stroke-linejoin="round" stroke-width="2" d="M19 9l-7 7-7-7"/></svg>
          </summary>
          <div class="px-6 pb-4 text-gray-600 text-sm leading-relaxed">
            All data is stored locally on your device and synced to your private iCloud container. We do not operate any servers. No data is shared with third parties (except anonymous subscription management via RevenueCat and address autocomplete via Apple MapKit). See our <a href="{{ '/privacy-policy/' | relative_url }}" class="text-fieldpad-600 hover:underline">Privacy Policy</a> for full details.
          </div>
        </details>

        <details class="group bg-white border border-gray-200 rounded-xl overflow-hidden">
          <summary class="flex items-center justify-between cursor-pointer px-6 py-4 font-semibold text-fieldpad-900 hover:bg-gray-50 transition-colors">
            How do I delete my data?
            <svg class="w-5 h-5 text-gray-400 group-open:rotate-180 transition-transform" fill="none" stroke="currentColor" viewBox="0 0 24 24"><path stroke-linecap="round" stroke-linejoin="round" stroke-width="2" d="M19 9l-7 7-7-7"/></svg>
          </summary>
          <div class="px-6 pb-4 text-gray-600 text-sm leading-relaxed">
            You can delete all data via <strong>Settings &gt; Data Management &gt; Delete All Data</strong> (requires double confirmation). Individual records can be deleted from their list views. EPA compliance logs within the 3-year retention period are protected per regulatory requirements. To remove iCloud data, go to <strong>iOS Settings &gt; Apple ID &gt; iCloud &gt; Manage Storage</strong>.
          </div>
        </details>

        <details class="group bg-white border border-gray-200 rounded-xl overflow-hidden">
          <summary class="flex items-center justify-between cursor-pointer px-6 py-4 font-semibold text-fieldpad-900 hover:bg-gray-50 transition-colors">
            What EPA regulations does FieldPad support?
            <svg class="w-5 h-5 text-gray-400 group-open:rotate-180 transition-transform" fill="none" stroke="currentColor" viewBox="0 0 24 24"><path stroke-linecap="round" stroke-linejoin="round" stroke-width="2" d="M19 9l-7 7-7-7"/></svg>
          </summary>
          <div class="px-6 pb-4 text-gray-600 text-sm leading-relaxed">
            FieldPad supports EPA Section 608 (ODS refrigerants in systems with 50+ lbs of charge) and AIM Act Subpart C (HFC refrigerants with GWP &gt; 53 in systems with 15+ lbs of charge, effective January 1, 2026). It tracks leak rate thresholds, repair deadlines, verification tests, chronic leaker reporting, and inspection schedules for both frameworks.
          </div>
        </details>

        <details class="group bg-white border border-gray-200 rounded-xl overflow-hidden">
          <summary class="flex items-center justify-between cursor-pointer px-6 py-4 font-semibold text-fieldpad-900 hover:bg-gray-50 transition-colors">
            What devices are supported?
            <svg class="w-5 h-5 text-gray-400 group-open:rotate-180 transition-transform" fill="none" stroke="currentColor" viewBox="0 0 24 24"><path stroke-linecap="round" stroke-linejoin="round" stroke-width="2" d="M19 9l-7 7-7-7"/></svg>
          </summary>
          <div class="px-6 pb-4 text-gray-600 text-sm leading-relaxed">
            FieldPad requires iOS 17 or later and runs on iPhone and iPad.
          </div>
        </details>

        <details class="group bg-white border border-gray-200 rounded-xl overflow-hidden">
          <summary class="flex items-center justify-between cursor-pointer px-6 py-4 font-semibold text-fieldpad-900 hover:bg-gray-50 transition-colors">
            I found a bug or have a feature request. How do I report it?
            <svg class="w-5 h-5 text-gray-400 group-open:rotate-180 transition-transform" fill="none" stroke="currentColor" viewBox="0 0 24 24"><path stroke-linecap="round" stroke-linejoin="round" stroke-width="2" d="M19 9l-7 7-7-7"/></svg>
          </summary>
          <div class="px-6 pb-4 text-gray-600 text-sm leading-relaxed">
            Email us at <a href="mailto:fieldpad@fieldpadpro.com" class="text-fieldpad-600 hover:underline">fieldpad@fieldpadpro.com</a> with a description of the issue or your feature idea. Screenshots are always helpful. We read every message and prioritize improvements based on user feedback.
          </div>
        </details>
